Ford Proud to Honor

The Ford Proud to Honor website was built as an homage to the Military. Ford Motor Company has been committed to honoring military veterans throughout the company's history. The website raises awareness, strengthens existing programs and creates an even more meaningful connection with our service members.

The website includes an e-commerce merchandise shop, links to special vehicle discounts for active military personnel and veterans, some military program promotions from our collaborators and a brief history of Ford Motor Company's commitment to veterans over the years. This website was originally built as a stand-alone site created using PHP and the Laravel framework, but has since been migrated to Ford's Corporate website and now lives within their Adobe Experience Manager CMS.
